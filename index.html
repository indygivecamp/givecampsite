Geeks giving back!

Every year, the Indianapolis tech/startup community gathers to help our non-profit community with their technical projects. We call it Indy GiveCamp.

Volunteer today

Who we are

Indy GiveCamp is a weekend-long collaboration between local developers, designers, database administrators, and non-profits. It is an opportunity for the technical community to celebrate and express gratitude for the contributions of these organizations by contributing code and technical know-how directly towards the needs of the groups.

When and where

November 1-3, 2013 at MID technologies 9600 Association Ct, Indianapolis, IN

Who we help

You! We are accepting non-profit proposals till September 30, 2013. If you are a deserving 501c3 organization with a technical need that can be accomplished in 48 hours, fill out our propsal form.

Get involved

Volunteer to help. Sponsor a meal. Share with a non-profit in need. There are lots of ways to help make this weekend a success.
